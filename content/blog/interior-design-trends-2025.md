# Interior Design Trends for 2025

The design world is evolving fast. Here are the top trends you can try right now with AI.

## 1. Quiet Luxury

Muted tones, natural materials, understated elegance. Less is more.

## 2. Biophilic Design

Bring the outdoors in. Plants, natural light, organic shapes.

## 3. Japandi

The perfect blend of Japanese minimalism and Scandinavian warmth.

## 4. Curved Furniture

Soft edges, rounded sofas, organic forms replacing sharp angles.

## 5. Earth Tones

Terracotta, warm browns, sage greens — colors from nature.
