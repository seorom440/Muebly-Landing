# The Future of Virtual Staging

Traditional virtual staging takes days and costs hundreds. AI is bringing that down to seconds and pennies.

## The Old Way

Traditional virtual staging meant hiring a professional, waiting 2-3 days, and paying €50–€150 per image. For a property with 10 rooms, that's easily €1,000 just to make empty rooms look livable.
