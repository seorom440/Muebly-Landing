## How We Evaluated AI Interior Design Tools

We tested 12 tools over 6 weeks across four categories:

1. **Output quality** — realism, shadow accuracy, scale correctness
2. **Speed** — time from upload to result
3. **Customization** — ability to control style, furniture, and room parameters
4. **Pricing** — cost per image and value for different user types

Each tool was tested with the same set of 20 room photos spanning living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, and home offices in varying lighting conditions.

## Category 1: AI Virtual Staging Tools

### Best for Real Estate: Muebly

Muebly's output quality scored highest in our shadow accuracy and scale tests. The platform is designed specifically for real estate and furniture retail use cases, with:

- Style selector with 12+ design categories
- Room dimension input for accurate furniture scale
- Multi-room batch processing
- Before/after export for listing use

**Price:** Free for first 3 rooms; plans from $9.99/month
**Speed:** 15–30 seconds per image
**Best for:** Real estate agents, property developers, interior designers

### Best for Speed: REimagineHome

REimagineHome prioritizes throughput over granular control. If you need 50 rooms staged in an hour, it delivers.

**Price:** From $29/month
**Speed:** Under 10 seconds
**Best for:** High-volume real estate brokerages

### Best for Customization: Spacely AI

Spacely offers the most granular control — you can specify furniture brand, material, color, and even request items from specific catalogs. Great for designers who need precise outcomes.

**Price:** From $49/month
**Speed:** 60–120 seconds (due to higher complexity)
**Best for:** Professional interior designers, furniture brands

## Category 2: AI Room Redesign Tools

These tools go beyond staging — they can redesign an occupied room, suggest renovations, or completely transform the aesthetic.

### Best Overall: Interior AI

Interior AI was the most versatile redesign tool we tested. Upload a furnished room and select a new style — the AI respects the room's architecture while completely replacing furniture and decor.

Standout features:
- 35+ design style presets
- Room "emptying" before redesign
- Wallpaper and flooring swap
- Commercial spaces (not just residential)

**Price:** From $29/month
**Best for:** Homeowners planning renovations, designers presenting concepts

### Best for AR Overlay: IKEA Kreativ

IKEA's AR tool combines a realistic room scan with their full furniture catalog. You can walk through your room with a virtual IKEA layout overlaid in real time. Limited to IKEA products, but the integration is seamless.

**Price:** Free (within IKEA app)
**Best for:** IKEA shoppers who want accurate placement before buying

## Category 3: AI Design Generation Tools

These tools generate room designs from scratch — from a text prompt, a mood board, or a floor plan.

### Best for Concept Generation: Midjourney + ControlNet

The combination of Midjourney for aesthetic generation and ControlNet for structural guidance produces aspirational design renders that no dedicated interior design tool currently matches for pure visual impact.

**Limitation:** Not photorealistic for real rooms; requires technical knowledge to use effectively.

**Best for:** Design inspiration, mood boards, concept presentations to clients

### Best AI for Floor Plan to 3D: Planner 5D

Planner 5D converts a floor plan (drawn in-app or uploaded) into a furnished 3D walkthrough. The AI automatically places furniture based on room type and dimensions.

**Price:** Free basic; from $7.99/month for AI features
**Best for:** Homeowners renovating, architects showing clients early-stage concepts

## Which Tool Should You Choose?

**If you are a real estate agent:** Muebly or REimagineHome — fast turnaround, listing-ready output, transparent per-image pricing.

**If you are an interior designer:** Spacely AI for client presentations; Midjourney for concept inspiration.

**If you are a homeowner planning a renovation:** Interior AI for redesign; Planner 5D for floor plan visualization.

**If you are a furniture retailer:** Muebly's retailer API or Spacely AI with custom catalog integration.

**If you just want to try it free:** Muebly (3 rooms free) or IKEA Kreativ (unlimited, but IKEA-only products).
